About

     I am a dedicated software engineer with over 2 years of professional experience bringing products through the software development life cycle. I have experience managing mid-sized teams across engineering, esports, game development, and marketing. I am passionate about creating thematic systems using games and web applications. I am currently studying Interactive Media and Computer Science at Michigan State University. I am student in Michigan State University's Game Design and Development program rated the #1 Undergraduate Game Development program at a public university and #7 overall by Princeton Review.
